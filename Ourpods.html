Our Romantic Pods

We provide luxurious pods for couples seeking a quiet romantic
getaway. It includes a king size bed, champagne and chocolates

The pod also includes a front garden which contains
BBQ facilities and a table for two to enjoy our scenic views.







Our Family Pods

Do you fancy a family glamping trip but also
with a bit of luxury? Lovely Laois Glamping Pods is the
ideal destination for your family needs. Our family

kitchen along with a kitchen table to enjoy a family dinner.
