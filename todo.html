Facilities On Site

Upon your arrival, you will be welcomed to your
2000 square foot glamping pod. Each pod has an
open space living room which consists of a sofa bed
and a small television. There is also a small kitchen located in
our pods which includes a microwave, a hob and a kettle.
Every pod has their own private bathroom which is equipped with
either a shower or a bath. Sit back and relax in your
private heated hot tub, an added bonus to your stay at Lovely Laois Glamping Pods. BBQ facilities can also be
arranged if pleased.







Activites Nearby

Lovely Laois Glamping Pods is surrounded by activites.
There are many things to explore that are close to
the campsite. A unique feature to our site is the surrounding
woodland area.Lovely Laois Glamping Pods are encompassed by
walking and cycling trails. There are many scenic landmarks
to view such as Brittas Lake, Emo Court, Capard Board walk ,
Stoney Man and many more. Plunge into the historical sites located nearby. Both Kinnity Castle and Birr Castle are located a short distance away.

For the guests seeking adventure, why not take a trip to Midlands Adventure Park where you will find rock-climbing, archery and ziplining.





Resturants nearby

To complete our pods, we are surrounded by an award
winning restaurant. The historic Bloomville Restaurant is located
just 3km away from our pods. They offer indoor and outdoor
dining and are happy to host any events you please. They
greet all customers with a warm welcome. They offer a wide range of flavoursome food which will leave your mouth watering.
Live music and beverages can be found nearby in the surrounding pubs. And if you decide you want to cook for your stay, there are also local shops within a walking distance.
