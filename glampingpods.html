Welcome to Lovely Laois Glamping Pods

Nestled away in the surroundings of the Slieve Bloom Mountains, Lovely Laois Glamping Pods are opened all year around with heated
and insulated pods. We promise you a luxurious stay in which a wide range of glamorous pods
are available for any occasion. Our pods are situated in the Slieve Bloom mountains. Surrounded by woods,
Lovely Laois Glamping Pods provides the ultimate tranquil experience. Immerse yourself in the beautiful nature of the Slieve Bloom
mountains, with The Rock of Dunamase, Glenbarrow and Emo Court located a short distance away.
